About Me

Aditya Chugh

Full Stack Developer & Teacher

Since my childhood, I've always had a very strong passion for computers. At the age of 12, I started writing applications in C#, and by the age of 14, I was developing design focused iOS apps. In June 2015, I was honored to have won a WWDC15 scholarship, and had the pleasure of meeting many bright minds, including Tim Cook and Craig Federighi, just to name a few. Lately I have been participating in various hackathons, and have attended some of Canada's most presitgious hackathons, including Hack the North and Hack Western.

I've always had a keen eye for design, and this has lead me to start my own design & development firm, Mindbend Studio. I have a strong passion for entrepeneurship, and plan to continue with this startup throughout the course of my post secondary education.

I strongly believe that the more you give to others, the more you achieve yourself. For this reason I have started a programming club at my school, I teach programming at a social programming club called Cipher, and I teach iOS and web development at an education startup, Spark Education. Throughout this journey, I have learned that I also have a strong passion for empowering others through programming, and that by increasing our generation's computer literacy, we can build a better future for everyone.
