Hi, I'm Aditya Chugh! I'm a second year student at the University of Toronto, studying Computer Science. When I'm not working on my startup TEAM.STUDY, I'm either working out, or at an autocross event. Did I mention I'm in love with BMWs and Porsches?

Being raised with a family business really shaped me as a person. I knew at a young age that I didn't want to work for some large corporation; I wanted to build one myself! Since then, I've worked on many projects, and even won a WWDC15 scholarship on behalf of Apple in the process. I'm currently building TEAM.STUDY, a colaborative note-taking platform for university students. Imagine Wikipedia, Github and Google Docs had a baby, that would be TEAM.STUDY!
